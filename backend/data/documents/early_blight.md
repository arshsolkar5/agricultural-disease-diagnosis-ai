# Early Blight (Alternaria solani)

## Overview
Early blight is a fungal disease affecting tomato, potato, and other solanaceous crops. Caused by *Alternaria solani*, it causes significant yield loss especially in warm, humid conditions.

## Symptoms
- Dark brown to black lesions on leaves with concentric rings (target spots)
- Lesions often appear on older leaves first
- Yellowing (chlorosis) around lesions
- Stem lesions can cause plant wilting
- Fruit may develop dark, sunken lesions near the stem end
- In severe cases, extensive defoliation occurs

## Conditions
- Temperature 20-30°C optimal
- High humidity and leaf wetness promote disease
- Poor air circulation increases risk
- Nutrient stress (especially potassium deficiency)
- Overhead irrigation spreads spores

## Treatment
### Chemical Control
- Chlorothalonil: 2-3g/L, spray every 7-10 days
- Copper-based fungicides: 2-3g/L, preventive sprays
- Mancozeb 75% WP: 2g/L for preventive sprays
- Azoxystrobin: 1-1.5mL/L, systemic action
- Iprodione 50% WP: 1.5g/L for curative action

### Cultural Control
- Remove infected leaves and plant debris
- Improve air circulation through proper spacing
- Avoid overhead irrigation, use drip irrigation
- Rotate crops (3-4 year rotation away from solanaceous crops)
- Use resistant varieties when available
- Ensure proper fertilization, especially potassium
- Stake plants to improve air flow
- Mulch to reduce soil splash

### Expected Recovery
- 7-14 days after chemical treatment if caught early
- 40-60% yield recovery if treated promptly
- Prevention more effective than cure
- Severe infections may cause permanent yield loss

## Cost Estimate
- Fungicide spray: ₹400-800/acre
- Labor: ₹150-250/acre
- Total: ₹550-1050/acre per application
- Multiple applications (2-3) typically needed: ₹1100-3150/acre total

## Prevention Tips
- Start preventive sprays before symptoms appear
- Monitor plants regularly, especially after rainy periods
- Maintain plant vigor through proper nutrition
- Remove lower leaves to improve air circulation
- Disinfect tools between plants
