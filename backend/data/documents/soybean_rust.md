# Soybean Rust (Phakopsora pachyrhizi)

## Overview
Soybean rust is a fungal disease affecting soybean plants, caused by *Phakopsora pachyrhizi*. It's a highly destructive disease that can cause rapid yield loss if not managed properly. The fungus is wind-dispersed and can spread over long distances.

## Symptoms
- Small, tan to reddish-brown lesions on leaves
- Lesions primarily on lower leaves initially
- Pustules (uredinia) erupting from lesions
- Yellowing (chlorosis) around lesions
- Premature defoliation in severe cases
- Reduced pod fill and seed quality
- Lesions may also appear on stems, pods, and petioles

## Conditions
- Temperature 15-28°C optimal for infection
- High humidity (>90%) and leaf wetness required
- Warm, humid weather promotes rapid spread
- Extended leaf wetness periods (6+ hours) critical
- Overhead irrigation increases risk
- Wind disperses spores over long distances
- Volunteer soybean plants serve as inoculum source

## Treatment
### Chemical Control
- Azoxystrobin: 1-1.5 mL/L, preventive sprays
- Pyraclostrobin: 1-1.5 mL/L, systemic action
- Tebuconazole 25% EC: 0.5 mL/L
- Propiconazole 25% EC: 0.5 mL/L
- Fluxapyroxad: 0.5-1 mL/L, preventive and curative
- Trifloxystrobin: 1-1.5 mL/L, systemic fungicide

### Cultural Control
- Plant early-maturing varieties to escape peak infection
- Maintain proper plant spacing for air circulation
- Avoid late planting in high-risk areas
- Remove volunteer soybean plants
- Rotate crops (1-2 years away from soybean)
- Avoid overhead irrigation when possible
- Monitor fields regularly during critical periods
- Use resistant varieties when available

### Expected Recovery
- 7-14 days after chemical treatment if caught early
- 50-70% yield recovery if treated promptly
- Prevention critical due to rapid disease spread
- Severe infections may cause 80%+ yield loss
- Early detection and immediate treatment essential

## Cost Estimate
- Fungicide spray: ₹700-1400/acre
- Labor: ₹250-350/acre
- Total: ₹950-1750/acre per application
- Multiple applications (2-3) typically needed: ₹1900-5250/acre total

## Prevention Tips
- Start preventive sprays at flowering stage
- Monitor fields regularly, especially after rain
- Scout lower leaves for early symptoms
- Use fungicides with different modes of action
- Avoid working with plants when wet
- Implement crop rotation practices
- Remove and destroy infected plant debris
- Stay informed about rust outbreaks in region
